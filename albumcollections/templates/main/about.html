Digital Record Shelves for your Spotify Albums

A metaphor for the purpose of this app

Imagine Spotify's role in your music listening experience in classic physical terms.

It is the record store (a very extensive and inexpensive one), the record player (with great features like turning the record over for you), and your friend telling you what new stuff to check out (who knows you weirdly well).

Spotify is also your strangely constructed record cabinet, providing you with infinite customizable shelves to store your singles (and ripped LP tracks) but only lending a single shelf with the appropriate dimensions to store your LPs.

Album Collections is a project that seeks to fill this organizational gap. It represents additional customizable shelves -- collections -- in your digital record cabinet. Whenever you're browsing the record store, you can take an album home with you to add to one of your collections. Then you can look through the albums on your shelves, rearrange them, pick out one to play or even stack them on your record player to spin one after the other.

The Mixtape has had a fantastic modern representation for a long time. This app introduces the concept of playlists for your albums, bringing album enjoyment into the music subscription age.


Guide

Using Album Collections requires a mix of actions between this app and Spotify. Below is a guide covering the main functionality of the app. Each section is labeled with an icon based on which app -- Album Collections and/or Spotify -- you will need to use to execute the action.

Install

Album Collections is a progressive web app. Install it on your device/s via a browser for the smoothest experience. Offline capabilities are not currently supported but are a possibility in the future.

Login

Press the Login button on the navbar or main page to authenticate with your Spotify account. This will allow you to view your Spotify playlists as collections and also enable collection playing and modifying.

Create a Collection

Make a new playlist in your Spotify Account that is both public and displayed on your profile (This should be the default for a new playlist). Then, add at least one album to it.

Add to a Collection

Navigate to the album you'd like to add to the collection. In the options for the album, select Add to playlist, and choose the playlist corresponding to the collection you would like it to be a part of. Alternatively in the desktop Spotify App, you can utilize the drag n' drop functionality to drag the album/s into the desired playlist.

View your Collections

The main page on Album Collections displays your collections. Click on a collection to open it in a new page. The collection page shows the cover art for all albums in the collections, and has a collection control bar at the bottom that shows the name of collection, along with several buttons to modify, play, or access it.

Play from a Collection

There are several ways to play music from a collection.

Play the playlist

Press the button in the collection control bar to take you to the Spotify playlist container for the collection. Then play.

Play a single album from the collection

Click on the album you would like to play. Then press the button to take you to the album in the Spotify app. Then play.

Play multiple albums in the collection

These actions may require opening up the Spotify app to allow the Connect API to discover the avaiable device.

These actions are also restricted to Premium Spotify users.

Play albums from start

Press the button on the collection control bar. As long as there is at least one avaiable device to play from, you will then be prompted to select a device to play on. Select the device you'd like to play on and hit .

This will:

  1. Turn off shuffle in your spotify account
  2. Create a playlist and add all albums in the collection to it
  3. Play the playlist from the start
  4. Delete the playlist (playback will still continue)
The above events will ensure that the albums are played in their correct order and that no tracks in the containing playlist that do not belong to an album get played.

Shuffle play albums

Press the button on the collection control bar. After, proceed as with Play albums from start. Behavior is identical except the temporary playback playlist will be shuffled by album.

Play starting at a particular album

Click on the album you would like to commence playback from, then press the button. After, proceed as with Play albums from start. Behavior is identical except the temporary playback playlist will be played starting from the chosen album.

Reorder a Collection

The order of the albums in the collection can be changed via drag n' drop. This functionality can be toggled using the button. The order of the collection on the collection page reflects that order of the containing playlist, and changes the order of playback (but won't change the playback order after it has already commenced -- see explanation of temporary playlist in Play albums from start).

Remove an Album from a Collection

Click on the album you would like to remove from the collection. Then press the button, and respond to the 'are you sure' prompt. This will remove all songs from this album from the containing playlist, and remove the album art image from the screen (but won't remove it from playback after it has already commenced -- see explanation of temporary playlist in Play albums from start).
